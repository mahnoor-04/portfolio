Hi I'm Mahnoor

Intro to coding

I first started coding when I was around 10, my dad forced me to use freecodecamp to start. I hated it (who likes being forced) and didn't learn anything. My dad realised this and bought a Udemy course by Angela Yu where I learnt basic HTML and CSS.

Giving Up

I didn't enjoy coding at all, It was like I was doing it as homework, I mentioned it to my dad so he asked me what I wanted to do and I decided on art. From here I started doing something I liked and I became good at it. Seeing this, my dad invested in ProCreate.

Graphic design

After finishing all my art classes my dad bought a course on Graphic Design and Design Aesthetics by Chris Do, I mastered it and decided I would enjoy making logos. I started simple and made a logo for myself and my dad, then two for TheNomadShop.

Re-introduced to coding

Three years later, I came across an amazing UX-UI design website and I thought I might be good at designing websites because I'm an Artist and I know what colors go well with what. During this time my dad had started another business.

MAJU

My family was rushed to Pakistan due to an emergency and we ended up staying there for 2 months because of it, my dad didn't want me to waste time so he got my mother to enroll me into a summer course of my choice. I chose WebDev

En-Route

Despite my bad history with coding, I enjoyed the web-dev summer course and practiced everyday. My dad was happy that I was finally enjoying coding and my Aunt supported me alot. My mum was busy but she liked that I was using my brain.
